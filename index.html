Sight Finder

Trabalho de IPM 2018/19

Descrição

Everybody makes plans before embarking on a holiday, especially if the location of it is new to us. One thing, usually, we all do, during the planning phase, is creating the travel itinerary, saving all the interest points we want and expect to visit.

For most people, the travel planning is as important as the trip itself. It’s already part of the experience of travelling. It’s during this phase that expectations rise and we cannot hold the excitement anymore. It’s like we’re in charge of our ship, and we’re ready to set sail to a new land. However, for some people, the “planning” is not so great. It’s boring. Finding museums, restaurants, festivals… Then we must find transportation, and all the problems that arise with scheduling anything. There are no platforms, no services specialized for creating a timetable with all the interest points we chose, accounting transportations.

Our objective is to provide our users a smooth, simple and intuitive way of streamlining the process of planning trips and sharing them.

Vai existir também uma pequena página onde os utilizadores vão poder ver algumas informações sobre os pontos de interesse: horas de abertura e encerramento, rating, informações históricas, fotos partilhadas, etc.
